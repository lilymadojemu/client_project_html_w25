Life Outside of the Classroom

Introduction

At the University of Michigan School of Information (UMSI), we understand the importance of maintaining a balance between one's school and personal life for overall academic success.This page goes over some of the opportunities and resources there are for enjoying your time outside of the classroom while at UMSI.

Professional & Personal Development

Workshops

  • Financial Empowerment Series
  • Food Literacy for All Series

Career & Education Counseling

Our professionally trained and experienced counselors work within a developmental framework to help individuals consider their options, make informed choices, and both define and resolve problems, while focusing on issues of achieving their career goals and balance between school, work, and family.
